Every year we provide opportunities for our students to develop their skills through real life authentic projects. These projects require problem solving, design, creativity, digital literacy and collaboration skills. These are all critical future skills we foster at Ryde Secondary College in preparing our students to become productive and successful citizens.

The Annual Fashion Show at Ryde Secondary College is a major collaborative project between the Technology & Applied Studies (TAS) and Creative & Performing Arts (CAPA) faculties. The theme this year reflects an interest in the arts as an inspiration for innovation.
